Redesigned- Department of Homeland Security website

Department of Homeland Security is a Government website where many green card holders.

The Problem

Stop Hate for Profit was created to hold social media companies accountable for hate on their platforms by having companies & individuals pledge & donate to the cause. After observing their website, I noticed that people are unable to donate at all(due to lack of donation page) & their website had so much unnecessary content it deters users from wanting to sign up completely.

The Solution

Our goal is to develop a trusted, user friendly & simplistic redesign for users to gain knowledge of what "Stop Hate for Profit" is about & be able to donate & sign up for their cause.
