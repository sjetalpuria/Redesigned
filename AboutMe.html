About Me

Who is she?

She is a User designer and developer based in California,USA. Her passion is to interact with users, help to design simple, attractive yet intuitive user-interacted products with excellent collaboration skills with other people.

Her story :-

She is a creative thinker ever since she was child. She was directed more towards art projects during her school. Along with art, she also got interested in computer science and pursued her academic interest. After earning degree in computer science was wondering how to merge both of her passions? After brainstorming the electives she came across the UX/UI field. This is the perfect field for her as both design and coding is involve in it. And now, she is ready to help users for their pleasant experience throughout the process. By using her skills, she can create better user experience and can also collaborate with the developers easily to help them troubleshoot the problems.
