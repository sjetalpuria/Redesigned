Redesigned-Stop Hate for Profit Website

Stop Hate for profit is a Website that protests against the hate that is spread by the people on social media. I examined the website and found out some minor and major missings like donation page, invisible links, unorganization of content etc. I used Adobe XD for designing and Visual studio code for Html/css/js code.

The Problem

Stop Hate for Profit was created to hold social media companies accountable for hate on their platforms by having companies & individuals pledge & donate to the cause. After observing their website, I noticed that people are unable to donate at all(due to lack of donation page) & their website had so much unnecessary content it deters users from wanting to sign up completely.

The Solution

Our goal is to develop a trusted, user friendly & simplistic redesign for users to gain knowledge of what "Stop Hate for Profit" is about & be able to donate & sign up for their cause.

Survey

I used google forms to create the survey and send it out to various age groups and gathered the data as shown below. When interviewing and surveying our users, I found out that many people experienced hate on social media especially on facebook. According to the survey, 62% people were unable to find the donation page in the actual website. And facebook and instagram are one of the highest social media apps were hate speech spreads the most.
